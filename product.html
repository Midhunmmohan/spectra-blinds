Blackout Shades

Blackout Blinds are really a great option for those of you struggling to rest. California Shutters

Traditionally, all plantation shutters were made from real wood, and the species of wood most commonly used was basswood. We've kept that tradition alive, and continue to manufacture both stained and painted wood in our facility in Miami Gardens. Many of our customers ask "with the benefits of your synthetic shutter, why would I ever buy a wood shutter?," and that's a great question. We do all of the finish work on our wood shutters here in our facility, which gives us the flexibility to stain or paint wood shutters any color under the sun. Do you have new stained kitchen cabinets you want to match? No problem! Do you fancy a very particular shade of white for all of the moldings in your home? No problem! We can custom match any color so that your shutters fit seamlessly into the color and style of your home.

Roller Shades

Roller Blinds, or also known as Holland Blinds, is an extremely popular and modern blind, suitable for all homes. They are a practical blind for everyday use, with a metal or plastic chain available, that operates the blind through an aluminium tube to roll up and down. During the daytime, the shades will provide some privacy. If you are looking for a roller shade that provides both privacy and complete light control we recommend blackout roller shades. Blackout shade fabrics block all the light from passing through the fabric of the roller shades.

Zebra Shades

Zebra Blackout – Blockout twin blinds are the premier choice for privacy. Blockout blinds completely seal out the sunlight and any prying eyes, making them the perfect solution for bedrooms and any other private areas. Zebra Translucent – Our light filtering translucent blinds are the perfect choice for any areas of your home where you would like a bit of extra light, but would also like protection from the sun. These blinds act as a sunlight filter, letting in just the right amount of light to fill your room with an ambient glow.
